Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Section 01 Intro

Introduction

Welcome to Pairly. Your privacy — and your relationship's privacy — matters deeply to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Pairly, the bonding app designed exclusively for couples.

By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. Pairly is built on trust, just like every great relationship.

Section 02 Data

Information We Collect

a. Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Name and display name
  • Email address
  • Password (securely encrypted)
  • Partner connection details (your paired partner's ID)

b. Shared Couple Content

To power your shared Pairly experience, we collect and store:

  • Messages and notes shared between partners
  • Doodles, drawings, and creative content you exchange
  • Moods and mood history you log together
  • Virtual plant and animal care activity data
  • Question-and-answer responses and quiz game results
  • Gifts and digital surprises sent within the app

c. App Usage Data

  • Feature usage patterns to improve your experience
  • Device type and operating system (iOS only)
  • Crash reports and diagnostics

d. Payment Information

Subscriptions are processed through the Apple App Store. We do not store or access your payment details — payment data is handled securely by Apple.

Section 03 Usage

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account and couple pairing
  • Deliver messages, doodles, moods, and gifts to your partner in real time
  • Keep track of your virtual plant and animal companions
  • Personalise your daily questions and game suggestions
  • Save your shared memories and milestones
  • Provide customer support when you need us
  • Process subscriptions and manage premium features
  • Improve app performance and bonding features
  • Send relevant notifications (if enabled by you)
Section 04 Security

Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data and your relationship's private content, including:

  • Encrypted storage of account credentials and messages
  • Secure servers with restricted access
  • Industry-standard HTTPS communication
  • Access limited strictly to your paired couple

No system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. We do, however, implement industry-standard practices to safeguard your data and your shared moments.

Section 05 Sharing

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Your intimate couple content is never shared with third parties for marketing or advertising. We may share data only:

  • With trusted service providers (hosting, analytics, push notification services)
  • If required by applicable law
  • To protect the rights or safety of users or third parties
Section 06 Deletion

Account Deletion

You may request account deletion at any time by:

  • Using the in-app "Delete Account" option in Profile & Settings
  • Contacting us at support@pairly.app

When your account is deleted, your personal data, messages, doodles, moods, and all shared couple content will be permanently removed from our servers.

Section 07 Children

Children's Privacy

Pairly is intended for adults in romantic relationships. This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Section 08 Updates

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on our website. We will notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notice.

Section 09 Contact

Contact Us
